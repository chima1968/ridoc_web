Appetizers

  • Caprese Salad

    Fresh tomatoes, mozzarella, and basil drizzled with balsamic glaze.

    Bruschetta

    Crispy slices of toasted bread topped with a mixture of diced tomatoes, garlic, fresh basil, and olive oil. Sometimes garnished with balsamic glaze for a tangy finish.

    Stuffed Mushrooms

    Button mushrooms filled with a savory mixture of breadcrumbs, garlic, cheese, and herbs, baked until golden and tender.

    Spring Rolls

    Crispy rolls filled with a mix of vegetables, noodles, and sometimes meat or shrimp, wrapped in thin rice paper or dough and deep-fried. Often served with sweet chili or soy dipping sauce.

Main Courses

  • Grilled Salmon

    Salmon fillet seasoned and grilled to perfection, served with lemon butter sauce.

    Grilled Steak

    A juicy cut of beef, seasoned with salt, pepper, and sometimes garlic or herbs, grilled to perfection. Often served with a side of mashed potatoes, roasted vegetables, or a rich sauce like peppercorn or chimichurri.

    Chicken Alfredo

    Tender pieces of grilled or sautéed chicken served over fettuccine pasta, coated in a creamy Alfredo sauce made with Parmesan, butter, and cream.

    Roasted Salmon

    A flaky, flavorful salmon fillet seasoned with herbs, lemon, and olive oil, roasted to perfection. Often accompanied by rice or a fresh green salad.

Desserts

  • Chocolate Fondue

    Assorted fruits and marshmallows served with rich chocolate fondue.

    Tiramisu

    A classic Italian dessert made of layers of coffee-soaked ladyfingers and mascarpone cream, dusted with cocoa powder. Its name means "pick me up" in Italian due to its coffee content.

    Cheesecake

    Assorted fruits and marshmallows served with rich chocolate fondue.

    Crème Brûlée

    A French custard dessert with a caramelized sugar crust on top. The silky custard is made with vanilla, eggs, and cream, offering a perfect contrast of textures.
